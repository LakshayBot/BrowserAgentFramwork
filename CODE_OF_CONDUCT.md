# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the project maintainers at conduct@browser-agent.dev.

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
